## Weather App

The **Weather App** provides users with real-time weather information. You can search by city or get weather details for your current location. The app also offers a 5-day forecast and the ability to toggle between Celsius and Fahrenheit.

### Features:
- **Search for Weather**: Enter a city to view current weather and a 5-day forecast.
- **Current Weather Display**: Shows temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather condition.
- **5-Day Forecast**: View the high and low temperatures for the next 5 days.
- **Geolocation**: Get weather for your current location.
- **Unit Toggle**: Switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit.
